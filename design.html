Bungalow Design: Features

Feature Description
Single-story layout All rooms are located on one floor, making it convenient for families and the elderly.
Open floor plan Offers a spacious feel with living, dining, and kitchen areas interconnected.
Low-pitched roof Bungalows typically feature a low-pitched roof for a sleek, modern look.
Large windows Maximizes natural light and offers expansive views of the surroundings.
Front porch Often includes a large, welcoming front porch, ideal for outdoor relaxation.
Wide eaves Extends beyond the walls, providing extra protection from the elements.
Built-in storage Efficient use of space with built-in cabinets and storage areas.
Accessibility With no stairs, bungalows are accessible for people with mobility issues.
Energy efficiency Due to their size and layout, bungalows are easier and cheaper to heat and cool.
Outdoor connection Bungalows often have large yards, creating a seamless indoor-outdoor living experience.
